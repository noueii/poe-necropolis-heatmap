    Necropolis Grave

    +X to Y Modifier Tier Rating

    +X to Y Modifier Tier Rating is the calculation method for reducing Y tags，(Raising Modifier Tier Rating excludes lower tier Modifiers from rolling, distributing their roll chance among higher tier Modifiers of the same Modifier type)，formula 1 - 100/(X + 100)。

    X
    cut rate
    50 1 - 100/(50+100) = 33%
    100 1 - 100/(100+100) = 50%
    200 1 - 100/(200+100) = 66%
    300 1 - 100/(300+100) = 75%
    400 1 - 100/(400+100) = 80%

    +100 will cut off the bottom 50% of the Y tag's affixes, +200 will cut off the bottom 66% of the Y tag's affixes, and so on. The weight of the reduced affix will become 0.

    Y Modifiers are X% scarcer

    Y Modifiers are X% scarcer (Modifiers that are 100% scarcer have weighting halved), formula 100/(X + 100)

    X
    scale
    100 100/(100+100) = 50%
    300 100/(300+100) = 25%

    X% increased chance of Y Modifiers

    Formula (X+100)/100。

    X
    scale
    100 (100+100)/100 = 200%
    300 (300+100)/100 = 400%
